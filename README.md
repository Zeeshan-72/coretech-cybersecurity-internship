## What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, networks, systems, and data from cyber attacks and unauthorized access.

## Main Types of Cyber Threats

- Malware
- Phishing
- Ransomware
- Denial of Service (DoS/DDoS)
- Password Attacks
- Social Engineering

## CIA Triad

### Confidentiality
Protecting data from unauthorized access.

### Integrity
Ensuring data remains accurate and unchanged.

### Availability
Making systems and data accessible when needed.

## What is Ethical Hacking?

Ethical hacking is the authorized process of identifying and fixing security vulnerabilities in systems.

## How Cybersecurity Helps CoreTech Innovation

Cybersecurity protects sensitive data, prevents attacks, maintains customer trust, and ensures business continuity.
